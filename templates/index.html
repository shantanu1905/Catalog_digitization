Catalog Digitization

The Catalog Digitization project aims to enhance product catalogs using advanced technologies like image and voice recognition. It allows users to easily add products through barcode scanning, image recognition, or voice commands, with support for Indic languages. Users can also manually input or modify product details. The app streamlines catalog management and enables easy exporting to CSV formats for external use, improving accessibility and efficiency for sellers.
